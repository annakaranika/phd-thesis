\subsection{Summary}
\label{comesh:section:conclusion}

\sysname{} shows that it is feasible and practical to build a fully-decentralized version of the Sense-Trigger-Actuation control plane.
Trace-driven experiments show \sysname{} keeps response latencies low, scales with the number of devices and routines, reduces bandwidth  10$\times$ vs. a central hub, 
and tolerates churn and medium mobility. Today's emerging IoT standards, like Matter~\cite{csa2025matter}, Thread~\cite{thread2020fundamentals}, and more, are a match for \sysname's vision of
a truly autonomous control plane for emerging mesh-based edge environments. {Future work can explore Byzantine faults, extreme mobility (e.g., vehicular networks), and super-large networks (e.g., cities).}
